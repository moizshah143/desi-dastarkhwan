where passion meets tradition

In 2010, we opened our small eatery in Lahore with a simple belief: food should taste like it came straight from an Ammi's kitchen. No shortcuts, no artificial flavours — just generations‑old recipes, fresh spices, and coal‑fired cooking.

Today, Desi Dastarkhwan has become a destination for those who crave authentic Pakistani taste, from the busy streets of Gulberg to your home through our delivery.
